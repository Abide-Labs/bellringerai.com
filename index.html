Features

Everything needed to keep dealership response tight.

No playful AI gimmicks. Just the operational jobs that protect lead flow and keep your team informed.

Instant lead response

Responds the moment a call, text, or form lead arrives so the first touch does not wait on staffing.

Missed call recovery

Turns missed calls into active follow-up instead of dead air, voicemail clutter, or sticky-note callbacks.

Appointment setting
